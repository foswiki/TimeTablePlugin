#VarTIMETABLE
---+++ TIMETABLE -- add a timetable to a topic
   * The =%<nop>TIMETABLE%= variable is handled by the TimeTablePlugin.
   * Syntax: =%<nop>TIMETABLE{attr1="value" attr2="value"}%=
   * Example:
     <verbatim>
%TIMETABLE%
   * Mon - 12pm - 1pm - Lunch with Marry
     </verbatim>
   * Related: VarTTCM, VarTTTOPICSETUP, TimeTablePlugin, CalendarPlugin, HolidaylistPlugin
